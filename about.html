My photography is primarily concerned with moments that are forever suspended between a ‘before’ and ‘after’ – elliptical, impregnated moments that remain unresolved. My photographs (re)present stories found in the enormous reservoir of psychological anxieties repressed beneath the seemingly calm surface.

The resulting world is one of “pure acts, unimportant in themselves,” preparing the way for “the sudden dazzling revelation of their meaning”. (Roberto Rossellini)
