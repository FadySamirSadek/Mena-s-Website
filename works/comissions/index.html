Welcome to Malaika’s ‘Cosmic Dream’ -- a technicoloured land of playful prints, shimmering threads and whimsical embroidery. Prepare to be whisked away to far away lands and distant universes, from Ancient Egyptian temples to Cocteau’s late nights in Cairo.

We’re dreaming of a year of gatherings with friends, lots of impromptu dancing, and tables dressed in our dazzling cloths with toppled glasses, spilt red wine and lots of good memories made.
