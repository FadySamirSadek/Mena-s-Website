I dream of a new age of curiosity. We have the technical means, the desire, and the people to make it happen. Why do we suffer then? Narrow discourses and lack of paths. In my portrait work, I shoot artists from my community who, at least at the moment of being photographed, are driving new curiosities in their respective fields. All the work here is uncommissioned.
